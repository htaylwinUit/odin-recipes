Cute Cupcake

Cute small cake

Consuming some cake or bringing a cupcake with you on the road
will give you the energy you need to get through the rest of the day!

Ingredients

  • 250g salted butter, at room temperature
  • 300g castor sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla essence
  • 4 large eggs
  • 270g self-raising flour
  • 250ml milk

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 180 degrees celsius for conventional ovens
    or 160 if you have a fan-forced oven.
  2. Line 2 x 12-hole muffin tins with cupcake wrappers.
    Cream the butter, sugar, and vanilla together until light and fluffy about 8-10 minutes.
  3. Gradually add the eggs one at a time and beat well in between each addition.
  4. Sift over the flour and add the milk gradually and beat again.
  5. Fill the cupcake wrappers â…” full and bake in the preheated oven for
    12-15 minutes or until golden and a skewer comes out clean.
    Allow them to cool completely.
  6. And decorate as you like with coffee, chocolate, funfetti.
